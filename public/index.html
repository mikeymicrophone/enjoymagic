Magic is widely considered the best game in the world.

There are several ways to play - and they stimulate many parts of your intellect as well as your aesthetic wisdom.

Finding a way to win, or an interaction that leads to advantages, is incredibly fun. Many can enjoy this low-tech game for hours on end.

Magic doesn't have to be hard to learn, but I was totally disappointed with the lessons I could find.

So, I decided to write a short book to help people learn not only how to play -- but how to enjoy Magic cards.

This week, I will release How to Enjoy Magic Cards, a $2 PDF about how to skip the annoying parts of Magic's culture and wisely spend your time (and money) on the fun parts.
